<p data-end="881" data-start="579">Joints are the connections between bones that allow movement and flexibility. <p data-end="923" data-start="883">Common symptoms of joint issues include:</p>

<li data-end="1047" data-section-id="wlv091" data-start="1021">Swelling or inflammation</li>
<li data-end="1088" data-section-id="1ovwuwo" data-start="1048">Difficulty performing daily activities</li>
</ul>
<p data-end="1188" data-start="1090">Ignoring these symptoms can lead to worsening conditions, making early support and care essential.</p>
